A new generation of Veterinary Practice Management

Features

Vetebase is a full featured application that allows Veterinarians and their staff focus on what matters most, their patients. Our aim is to provide the best software that gets out of your way and lets you focus on your practice.

Patient Profiles

Create, manage, and update your patients' information, treatment plans, notes, and much more.

Appointments

Manage your practice's appointments and communicate with your clients.

Multi-Practice Management

Have more than one practice? Vetebase allows you to manage all of them from on application.

Practice Whiteboard

Show which patient is in each of your exam rooms and how long they have been in the room.

Inventory Management

Running a veterinary practice involves managing an inventory of medicine, hardware, and supplies.

Staff Scheduling

Schedule and track your staff's schedule.
